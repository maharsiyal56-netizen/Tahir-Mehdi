About Me

Hi, I’m Tahir Mehdi, a passionate Front-End Developer with over five years of hands-on experience crafting visually appealing and user-friendly websites and web applications. I specialize in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and excel at building dynamic interfaces with modern frameworks like React.

Currently, I’m pursuing a Bachelor’s in Computer Science (BSCS) at the University of Southern Punjab, Multan, where I continue to sharpen my skills and stay up-to-date with the latest web technologies. My goal is to blend creativity with clean, efficient code to deliver seamless digital experiences.

My approach combines technical expertise with an eye for design to create digital experiences that are both visually appealing and highly functional. I believe in writing clean, maintainable code and constantly learning new technologies.

When I’m not coding, you can find me reading novels or exploring new skills.
