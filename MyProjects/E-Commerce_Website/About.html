From Garage to Global

TechStore began in 2015 as a small startup in a Silicon Valley garage. Our founders, Alex Chen and Maria Rodriguez, shared a passion for technology and a vision to make the latest gadgets accessible to everyone.

What started as an online store selling refurbished smartphones quickly grew into a comprehensive electronics retailer. Today, we serve millions of customers worldwide with a catalog of over 10,000 products from top brands.

Despite our growth, we've stayed true to our founding principles: offering quality products, competitive prices, and outstanding customer service.
